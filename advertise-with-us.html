Advertising On The Shoppers Website

This page provides information about advertising on The Shopper Website. Our priced packages can be found on the Pricing Plan page.

If you require any additional information, please contact our sales team.

Information about The Shoppers Website

• The Shoppers Website is an online search engine for businesses to promote their products and services to customers. Customers search using category, product or services searches or by looking for specific businesses.

• Our selling point to customers is that by visiting our site they can quickly and easily find the products, services or businesses that they are looking for, without spending time searching aimlessly on generic search engines. We have designed the site so that it's easy to use, caters for both customers that are looking for specific products, services or businesses and those who are just browsing, and promotes the businesses advertising with us so as to optimise their impact.

Companies that use The Shoppers Website

• The Shoppers Website is for any sized business selling products or services to the general public. With our growing popularity and calibre of advertisers, The Shoppers Website is very quickly becoming an essential marketing tool for successfully attracting customers.

How it works

• All our packages (except for the Basic Package), provides companies with space for their own static or animated logo or banner. Customers will click on the advert to be taken directly through to your company website.

• Our search engine and category linked pages will ensure that customers will be directed through to where your advert is hosted on The Shoppers Website. This simple yet effective process ensures that shoppers have a hassle-free experience and your company will attract click-through from genuine customers.

Customers continue returning to use our site, as our offer is easy-to-use and provides them with a free service that generates the results they are looking for.

Timescales

• Once we have received your completed order, your advert will be posted within two working days.

• The Shoppers Website has thousands of businesses selling everything from high street fashion and exclusive holidays to discounted electrical goods and top-end jewellery. Whatever you're looking for, and with products and services for all budgets, we are the only site you need to find the exact shops for you.Yy

Advertising Space

• Your company can either advertise in one category or select a number of categories. For example, if you are a fashion retailer, you may want to advertise in our Women, Men and Kids categories, using different adverts in each. We can then link the click-through to these specific areas on your site. This will encourage customer retention.

• Our website allows you to be dynamic with your sales strategy. You can update your advert at any time for £30. You can use this facility to highlight special promotions, time limited discounts, sales, and seasonal deals.

Linking to your company website

• When a customer clicks on your advert, your website will open up in a new window. Our design makes the site easy to navigate and will intuitively direct customers to the businesses that are most appropriate to their search. Your company therefore has the potential to be seen by large numbers of your target customers who are specifically looking for what you provide.

Company Name Search Directory

• When you sign up for a Package your company will automatically be listed in our Company name Search directory. Customers who are looking for your company will use our search function, and will either be directed straight to your website or your Company Details Page, which ever you provide.

Formats

• All animated logos and banners need to be provided in HTML code. All static images should be provided in a jpeg or gif file format.

Company's without websites

• If you don't have a website, your advert will link to the Company Details Page on our site, which will contain your contact information and we will give you 500 characters to detail what your company does.
